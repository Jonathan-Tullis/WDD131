January 15

By Sarah Mitchell

Fiction • Adventure

The Midnight Library

A thought-provoking exploration of life's infinite possibilities. Matt Haig crafts a story that resonates deeply with anyone who has ever wondered about the paths not taken. The protagonist's journey through alternate realities is both heartbreaking and uplifting, reminding us that every choice shapes our existence in profound ways.

January 8

By James Chen

Mystery • Thriller

The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides delivers a psychological thriller that keeps you guessing until the very last page. The story of Alicia Berenson, a woman who stopped speaking after allegedly murdering her husband, is masterfully woven with unexpected twists. A gripping read that explores the depths of human psychology and the secrets we keep.

December 28

By Emily Rodriguez

Fantasy • Young Adult

The House in the Cerulean Sea

TJ Klune's enchanting tale is a warm embrace in book form. Following a social worker who discovers a magical orphanage, this story celebrates found family, acceptance, and the courage to be yourself. With charming characters and a heartwarming plot, it's the perfect comfort read that will leave you smiling long after the final page.

December 15

By Michael Thompson

Non-Fiction • Science

Educated

Tara Westover's memoir is a powerful testament to the transformative power of education. Her journey from growing up in a survivalist family in rural Idaho to earning a PhD from Cambridge is both inspiring and harrowing. A beautifully written exploration of family, identity, and the pursuit of knowledge that challenges everything you think you know about resilience.
